Established in 2018 by Dr. Ely Jesse P. Legaspi, the EJPL Dental Clinic embodies a commitment to quality dental care and community service. Driven by a passion to serve his hometown of Bacoor City, Cavite, Dr. Legaspi meticulously built the clinic from the ground up.

Our dedicated team, led by Dr. Legaspi and supported by five skilled assistants, ensures the clinic operates seamlessly. From managing clinic maintenance to instrument sterilization and patient care, our team works diligently to provide a comfortable and efficient experience for every visitor.

Specializing in a wide range of services, including dental consultations, oral prophylaxis, extractions, braces installation, impacted wisdom tooth removal, jacket crown fittings, and prosthetic work, we strive to meet the diverse needs of our patients.

At EJPL Dental Clinic, we maintain an open-door policy and prioritize convenience by scheduling appointments daily to accommodate our clients' availability.
